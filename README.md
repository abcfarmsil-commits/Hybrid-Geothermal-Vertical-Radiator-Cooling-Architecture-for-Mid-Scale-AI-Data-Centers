# ABCFARMSIL - Modular Infrastructure Systems

**Modular agricultural and energy infrastructure for distributed production systems**

---

## Overview

ABCFARMSIL designs and implements modular infrastructure systems that enable distributed food production, energy recovery, and site activation. Our systems are infrastructure-grade, deterministic, and designed for multiple applications across food production, energy systems, aquaculture, and data operations.

### Core Philosophy

- **Infrastructure First**: Systems are designed as buried/fixed infrastructure, not surface-level equipment
- **Modular Architecture**: Components can be deployed independently or integrated into larger systems
- **Multi-Application**: Food production is one application, not the sole business model
- **Deterministic Operation**: Systems provide predictable, measurable outcomes with clear ROI

---

## System Portfolio

### 1. Core Growing Systems
- **Modular Rack Systems**: 16-20 level vertical growing infrastructure with integrated LED lighting and mist irrigation
- **Germination Engines**: Tray-based biological reactors with environmental control
- **Production Modules**: Scalable units from 2-rack pilot to full-scale deployment

### 2. Energy Systems
- **LUMENFIELD Hydro Battery Module**: Gravity-based energy recovery infrastructure (15-22 kWh/day, 2-3 tonnes CO2e/year offset)
- **Geothermal Integration**: Owner-built geothermal systems cost-competitive with conventional HVAC
- **Grid-Interactive Systems**: Distributed infrastructure with utility integration

### 3. Aquaculture Infrastructure
- **Yellow Perch Breeding Systems**: 53-foot reefer container-based hatchery and nursery
- **Modular Aquaculture Units**: Scalable breeding and grow-out infrastructure

### 4. Site Activation
- **Illinois Regional Site Readiness**: Temporary infrastructure for development-ready parcels
- **Utility Demand Generation**: Real utility data collection for site qualification
- **Development Support**: Infrastructure for economic development and site selection

### 5. Data & Operations
- **Uptake Integration**: Data infrastructure for operations and monitoring
- **MRV Systems**: Measurement, Reporting, and Verification for sustainability metrics
- **Telemetry & Control**: Remote monitoring and automated control systems
