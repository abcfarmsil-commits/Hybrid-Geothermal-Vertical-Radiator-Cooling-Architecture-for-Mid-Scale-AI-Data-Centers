# ABCFarmsil
## Harvest Freshness, Delivered Sustainably

## Executive Summary

ABCFarmsIL is building a network of self-contained, tech-enabled urban farms designed to deliver fresh, local, and pesticide-free produce directly to chefs and food businesses. Our plug-and-play units use advanced hydroponics, climate automation, and solar integration to grow crops year-round—anywhere in the city.

We're starting where demand is high: the 3,000+ chefs and food businesses in Chicago seeking reliable, transparent local produce. The founding team combines expertise in food systems, automation, and community organizing. Our seed round of $69,000 will allow us to launch our innovative container farm and begin serving high-profile chefs in Q1 2026.

ABCFarmsIL isn't just a farm—it's a scalable blueprint for how cities can feed themselves sustainably, affordably, and equitably.

## Problem Definition & Validation

### Problem Analysis

#### Whom?
Chicago's fine-dining chefs, country-club kitchens, and their diners who demand hyper-local, sustainably grown ingredients—but currently face significant challenges in sourcing them.

#### Causes
Traditional produce supply chains harvest on rural farms, ship through multiple distributors, and take 2-4 days to reach city kitchens. This results in:
- Diminished flavor and nutritional value
- Inconsistent quality and availability
- Excessive packaging waste
- High carbon footprint from transportation
- Disconnection between growers and chefs

#### Impact
Delayed, resource-heavy supply chains force chefs to accept:
- Lower flavor quality
- Up to 15% spoilage
- Inflated food costs
- Limited menu creativity
- Inability to meet customer demands for truly local ingredients

## Solution

Microgreens contain similar and often greater amounts of nutrients than mature greens. As such, they may similarly reduce the risk of the following diseases when included as part of a varied diet:

### Solution Features

#### Heart Disease
Microgreens are a rich source of polyphenols, a class of antioxidants linked to a lower risk of heart disease. Animal studies show that microgreens may lower triglyceride and "bad" LDL cholesterol levels.

#### Just-in-time harvest
Imagine a chef opening a delivery at 4pm - not of plastic clamshells with wilting greens, but living trays of vibrant microgreens still attached to their growing medium, harvested just hours before. This is the ABCFarmsIL difference.

#### Certain Cancers
Antioxidant-rich fruits and vegetables, especially those rich in polyphenols, may lower the risk of various types of cancer. Polyphenols have been linked to a lower risk of certain types of cancer.

#### Diabetes
Antioxidants may help reduce the type of stress that can prevent sugar from properly entering cells. In lab studies, fenugreek microgreens appeared to enhance cellular sugar uptake by 25-44%.

#### Zero-waste return system
We've engineered a closed-loop tray cycle so nothing goes to landfill: after each delivery, chefs simply leave used trays curbside for pickup and reuse, eliminating packaging waste while ensuring optimal growing conditions.

## Business overview

ABCFarmsIL is a tech-enabled urban agriculture company specializing in ultra-fresh microgreens delivered directly to chefs within hours of harvest. Our innovative approach combines controlled environment agriculture with a zero-waste return tray system, creating a closed-loop model that maximizes freshness while minimizing environmental impact.

Our core offering is the proprietary 6-square system that allows chefs to receive multiple microgreen varieties in a single tray, still living in their growing medium for maximum freshness and shelf life. This system eliminates packaging waste while providing chefs with unprecedented flavor, nutrition, and convenience.

The business follows a phased implementation approach, starting with a 5-rack growing system (Phase 0.8) and expanding to a network of container modules with diversified product offerings including specialty mushrooms and heritage eggs. Our technology integration includes QR tray-tracking, climate automation, and renewable energy systems.
