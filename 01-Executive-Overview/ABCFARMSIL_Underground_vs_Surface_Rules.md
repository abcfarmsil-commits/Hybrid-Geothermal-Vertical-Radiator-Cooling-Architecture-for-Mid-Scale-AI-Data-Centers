# ABCFARMSIL Underground vs Surface Rules
## The Decision Framework

---

## Core Principle

**The Core System is Infrastructure → It Goes Underground.**

**Everything Biological That Can Be Sealed → Goes Underground.**

**Everything That Requires Surface Access → Stays Above.**

---

## THE UNDERGROUND LAYER (Fixed Infrastructure)

### What Lives Underground: Non-Negotiable

**Module A: Germination Engines**
- Microgreens (all varieties)
- Plant production cycles
- Sealed, deterministic, auditable

**Module D: Harvest & Cold Chain**
- Harvest station
- Cold storage
- Packing infrastructure
- Sealed, environment-controlled

**Utility Spine**
- Water systems
- Energy buffering
- Monitoring & controls
- MRV systems

### Why Underground?
- Sealed environments → no contamination risk
- Stable temperatures → reduced energy costs
- No pollination → deterministic cycles
- No retail access → workflow focus
- Quiet, invisible → NIMBY-proof
- Infrastructure-grade → utility easements, ROW

### Decision Rule: If it's sealed, deterministic, and non-pollinated → Underground.

---

## THE SURFACE LAYER (Biological Necessity)

### What Lives Surface: Non-Negotiable

**Dome A (Plant-Only, Surface-Adjacent)**
- Pollinated crops
- Plants requiring pollination
- Can be buried if fully sealed, but surface-adjacent is practical

**Pollinators**
- Bees, butterflies
- Require outdoor access
- Cannot be sealed

### Why Surface?
- Pollination requires outdoor access
- Natural light integration
- Air exchange capability
- Regulatory clarity

### Decision Rule: If it needs pollination or outdoor access → Surface.

---

## THE MODULAR LAYER (Optional Tenants)

### What's Modular: Can Be Either Underground or Surface

**Module B: Fungi Production**
- Science-driven, sealed cycles
- Can be underground (preferred)
- Can be surface if needed

**Module C: Vermiculture & Soil Loop**
- Needs access to waste streams
- Can be underground or surface
- Modular based on facility design

**Aquaculture (Future)**
- Crayfish as biological tenants
- Can be underground (sealed tanks)
- Can be surface if integrated
- Optional, not core

### Why Modular?
- Optional, not essential to core system
- Can be deployed based on facility constraints
- Tenant-based, not owner-based
- Scalable based on demand

### Decision Rule: If it's optional or tenant-based → Modular (default underground, flexible surface).

---

## THE QUICK DECISION TREE

### Question 1: Does it need pollination?
- **YES** → Surface
- **NO** → Go to Question 2

### Question 2: Is it sealed, deterministic, and non-pollinated?
- **YES** → Underground
- **NO** → Go to Question 3

### Question 3: Is it optional/tenant-based?
- **YES** → Modular (default underground)
- **NO** → Surface (unless fully sealed)

---

## THE SURFACE-ADJACENT ZONE

### What's Surface-Adjacent: Hybrid Approach

**Dome A (If Fully Sealed)**
- Can be buried for thermal efficiency
- Can be surface-adjacent for access
- Decision based on facility constraints

### Decision Rule: Surface-adjacent = can be either, choose based on practical constraints.

---

## SUMMARY: THE THREE ZONES

| Zone | What Goes Here | Why | Mobility |
|------|----------------|-----|----------|
| **Underground** | Core infrastructure, germination engines, cold chain | Sealed, deterministic, non-pollinated, invisible, NIMBY-proof | Fixed |
| **Surface** | Pollinators, plants requiring pollination, outdoor access | Pollination needs, natural light, regulatory clarity | Fixed |
| **Modular** | Fungi, vermiculture, aquaculture, optional biologicals | Optional, tenant-based, scalable based on demand | Flexible |

---

## APPLICATION EXAMPLES

### Example 1: Should we add honeybees?
- Needs pollination? **YES**
- → **Surface** (non-negotiable)

### Example 2: Should we add crayfish tanks?
- Needs pollination? **NO**
- Sealed/deterministic? **YES**
- Optional/tenant-based? **YES**
- → **Modular** (can go underground, flexible)

### Example 3: Should we add microgreens?
- Needs pollination? **NO**
- Sealed/deterministic? **YES**
- → **Underground** (non-negotiable)

### Example 4: Should we add tomatoes?
- Needs pollination? **YES**
- → **Surface** (or fully-sealed surface-adjacent)
