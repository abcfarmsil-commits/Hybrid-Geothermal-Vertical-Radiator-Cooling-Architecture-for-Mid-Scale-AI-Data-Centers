## The Anti-Rejection Strategy

Here's how we break the cycle without overwhelming you:

### Step 1: Reframe "Rejection" as "Learning"

Old frame: "If they say no, I failed and the idea is bad"
New frame: "If they say no, I learned something valuable about the market"

Every "no" is data. Data is good. Data helps you improve.

### Step 2: Start With Zero-Risk Asks

Don't lead with "Will you buy my microgreens for $40/tray?"

Lead with "Can I ask you 3 questions about how you source fresh greens?"

- No rejection possible (everyone answers questions)
- You learn about the market
- You build relationships
- Sales conversations happen naturally from there

### Step 3: The "Free Sample" Model

This eliminates rejection completely:

"I'm launching a microgreens operation in Chicago. I'd love to give you a free tray to try. No obligation, just feedback. Would you be interested?"

- If they say yes: You get distribution + feedback
- If they say no: You learn who your customer isn't
- Either way: You learn, you win

### Step 4: Build Courage Through Action

The fear goes away AFTER you do the scary thing, not before.

First call: Scary
Second call: Less scary
Third call: Manageable
Tenth call: Just a call

Action creates confidence. Planning never does.
