## 2. Company Overview

### Mission & Vision

### Company History & Origin Story
ABCFarmsIL was founded to address a critical gap in the culinary supply chain that founder Jon James experienced firsthand: the lack of truly fresh, high-quality microgreens delivered at peak flavor. ## 5. Operations Plan

### Production Facilities
- **Phase 0.8**: Initial rack with 48 usable tray slots
- **Phase 1**: Second rack, doubling capacity to approximately 64 trays/week
- **Phase 2**: Geodesic dome for silkie chickens
- **Phase 3**: Separate facilities for mushroom cultivation and rabbit husbandry

### Production Process: Microgreens
1. **Seed Selection & Preparation**: Sourcing highest quality seeds, sanitization and pre-soaking as needed
2. **Growing Medium Preparation**: Proprietary blend of coco coir and amendments
3. **Seeding**: Precise density seeding for optimal growth
4. **Germination Phase**: 2-3 days in blackout conditions with optimal humidity
5. **Growing Phase**: Controlled light spectrum, temperature, and humidity
6. **Harvesting**: Cut at peak flavor point, never refrigerated
7. **Packaging**: Minimal, sustainable packaging with harvest date and variety information
8. **Delivery**: Direct farm-to-chef within hours of harvest

### Production Schedule
- **Staggered Planting**: Daily seeding to ensure consistent availability
- **Harvest Schedule**: Morning harvests timed for same-day delivery
- **Delivery Routes**: Optimized for efficiency and freshness
- **Quality Control**: Multiple checkpoints throughout process

### Operational Metrics
- Weekly output @ 85% utilization: 32 trays (Phase 0.8)
- Production capacity at full implementation: 400+ trays/month
- Labor efficiency: 18 minutes per tray (seeding to packaging)
- Germination success rate: >95%
- On-time delivery rate: >99%
- Water usage: 0.18-0.3 gallons per tray
- Energy usage: 15-20 watts per tray

### Supply Chain Management
- **Seed Suppliers**: Johnny's Selected Seeds, Baker Creek Heirloom Seeds

3. **Chef Feedback Loop**:

4. **Documentation & Traceability**:
