Got it — here’s the **exact parts-count checklist** for **2 racks**, each with **4 lit shelves**, **3 trays per lit shelf** (**24 trays total**) and **misting valves per tray**.

Your trays are **17.25" x 13"** (so **3 trays fit on a 45" shelf**).

---

## **Build target (what this list supports)**

* **2 racks**  
* **Per rack:** 4 lit shelves × 3 trays \= **12 trays**  
* **Total:** **24 trays**  
* **Misters:** **2 per tray** \= **48 misters**  
* **Control:** **1 valve per tray** \= **24 valves**  
* **Manifolds:** **1 per shelf** \= **8 shelf-manifolds** (gives clean modular plumbing)

---

# **A) Core plumbing \+ control (feeds both racks)**

### **Feed / split / control (at pump or source)**

* **1×** 3/4" GHT **2-way hose splitter** (individual shutoffs)  
  *One outlet per rack so either rack can run solo.*  
* **2×** 3/4" GHT **ball valve** (one per rack line) *(optional if your splitter already has shutoffs)*  
* **2×** **150-mesh** inline **filter**, 3/4" GHT (one per rack line)  
* **2×** **pressure regulator** (recommend **25–30 PSI**) 3/4" GHT (one per rack line)  
* **2×** **pressure gauge** 0–60 PSI, 3/4" GHT inline OR tee-mounted  
  *(Helps you repeat mist results every time.)*  
* **2×** set **quick-connects** (garden hose style) so racks can disconnect fast

### **If you’re feeding from the sprayer wand end**

Pick the adapter that matches your wand threads:

* If your wand end is **1/4" NPT male** → need **1/4" NPT female → 3/4" GHT male**  
* If your wand end is **1/4" NPT female** → need **1/4" NPT male → 3/4" GHT male**

*(This is exactly the “wand-end connection” idea you had — it’s the right place to add filter/regulator/gauge.)*

---

# **B) Per-rack distribution (clean \+ serviceable)**

### **Mainline on each rack (recommended: 1/2" poly)**

Per rack:

* **12–15 ft** of **1/2" poly tubing** (main riser/header up the rack)  
* **1×** 1/2" **elbow** (base turn)  
* **4×** 1/2" **tees** (one tee to feed each lit shelf manifold)  
* **1×** 1/2" **end cap** (top end)  
* **1×** **flush/bleed valve** at the *lowest point* (tee \+ small valve \+ short drain tube to bucket)

For **2 racks**, double that:

* **25–30 ft** 1/2" poly tubing total  
* **2 elbows**, **8 tees**, **2 end caps**  
* **2 flush/bleed valves**

---

# **C) Shelf manifolds (1 per lit shelf)**

You want a manifold that’s **full-flow/unregulated** (good for misters) and gives multiple outlets.

For **8 lit shelves total**:

* **8×** **6-port drip manifold** (use **3 ports**, cap the rest as spares)

Manifold mounting \+ inlet adapters:

* **8×** 1/2" **barb × 1/2" MPT** adapter *(to screw into manifold inlet if it’s 1/2" FPT)*  
* **1×** roll PTFE thread tape

Outlet caps (spares / future expansion):

* **24×** 1/4" **goof plugs / caps** (3 unused outlets per manifold × 8\)

If you buy **adjustable-flow** manifolds (valves built-in per port), you *can* skip the separate needle valves below — but most people still like having a true “tray valve” right at the tray line.

---

# **D) “Valve per tray” \+ lines to each tray (24 trays)**

### **Per tray (x24)**

* **1×** 1/4" **inline needle valve** (barb-barb) → **24 total**  
* **\~4 ft** of 1/4" tubing from manifold to tray average → buy **200 ft** roll (gives slack)

So get:

* **1×** roll **1/4" micro tubing, 200 ft**  
* **24×** 1/4" **inline needle valves**

Helpful small fittings:

* **24×** 1/4" **straight couplers** (insurance/spares)  
* **24×** 1/4" **tube clamps** or **mini zip ties** (optional but reduces leaks)

---

# **E) Misting hardware (48 misters total)**

### **Per tray (x24)**

* **2×** micro-misters \= **48 total**  
* **1×** 1/4" **tee** to split the tray line into left/right mister lines \= **24 total**  
* **2×** short 1/4" whip lines (\~8–12") from tee to each mister

So get:

* **48×** micro-misters (360° or 180° depending on placement)  
* **24×** 1/4" tees  
* **1×** pack of **mister stakes/clips** (48) OR mounting clips if you’re attaching to the rack/tray frame

**Placement tip (works great for your 17.25" tray length):**

* Put the two misters about **1/3 and 2/3** down the long axis, angled slightly inward.

---

# **F) Light mounting \+ drip protection (quick add-ons)**

* **Shelf liners** (wire shelves \+ water \= drip city)  
* **Black-out material** for the bottom “dark shelf” (you already plan this)  
* **Zip ties \+ adhesive cable/tube clips** (keeps tubing off LEDs and out of the way)

## **Optional: plumb the dark shelf for “emergency trays”**

* Add **1 shutoff valve** feeding that manifold (per rack) \= **2 valves**
