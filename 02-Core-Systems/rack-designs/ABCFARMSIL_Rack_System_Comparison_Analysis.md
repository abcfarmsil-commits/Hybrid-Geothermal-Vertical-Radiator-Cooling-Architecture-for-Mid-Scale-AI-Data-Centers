## Recommendations

### Primary Recommendation: **Continue with Custom Design**

**Rationale:**

1. **Superior Cost Efficiency:** Your custom design offers the best cost per tray at $10.94-$11.72 compared to $201-$266 for pre-built systems.

2. **Maximum Vertical Utilization:** 16-20 levels vs. 3-4 levels in commercial systems, optimizing your facility footprint.

3. **Complete Customization:** Your design integrates specific features:
   - Mist irrigation system (vs. NFT in CropKing)
   - Custom LED spacing and spectrum
   - Drip guards and water management
   - IP-protected design elements

4. **Scalability:** Design allows for phased implementation and easy replication

5. **Total System Control:** Full ownership of design and ability to iterate improvements

### Alternative: **Hybrid Approach**

Consider purchasing pre-built components while maintaining custom assembly:

- **Buy:** LED lighting systems from GrowRack suppliers
- **Buy:** Commercial-grade casters and wheels
- **Buy:** Professional irrigation components
- **Build:** Custom frame and mounting system

**Benefits:**
- Reduced assembly complexity
- Higher quality components
- Still maintain customization flexibility
- Moderate cost increase (~20-30% over full DIY)

### Consider Pre-Built If:

- **Time is critical:** Need immediate deployment
- **Budget allows:** 3-4x cost is acceptable
- **Scale is smaller:** Only need 1-2 racks initially
- **Technical expertise limited:** DIY assembly not feasible
- **Warranty/support priority:** Commercial support needed
