## What it is: A turnkey, reversible pilot that deploys buried regenerative utility infrastructure producing chef-selected microgreens (and optional specialty mushrooms) on-property—delivered to your kitchen within hours, not days.

## Why resorts say yes: lower plate-cost volatility vs imported product, higher menu freshness/consistency, strong guest story, and measurable sustainability wins—with minimal operational burden on your team. **Buried infrastructure eliminates visual impact and provides exclusive, secure production capability.**

---

## Pilot Structure

- **Duration:** 90 days (extendable).
- **Footprint:** Underground utility space or surface-adjacent BOH pad (approx. 12'×20' to 12'×30', depending on output and deployment configuration).
- **Utilities:** Standard power + water (or we can bring buffer tanks / power staging if needed).
- **Outputs:** Weekly delivery cadence matched to your menus (microgreens always available; optional mushrooms by availability).
- **Operations:** ABCFARMSIL operates and maintains the unit; your team simply orders + receives into kitchen workflow.
- **Infrastructure Option:** Fully buried deployment available for underground utility corridors, with surface-adjacent integration for harvesting access.

---

## Deployment Configurations

### Option 1: Surface-Adjacent BOH Placement
- Discreet BOH pad or service-yard corner
- Above-grade or semi-buried configuration
- Outdoor-style production environment
- Standard deployment timeline: 5-7 days

### Option 2: Buried Infrastructure Deployment
- Underground utility corridor or vault integration
- Fully sealed, deterministic production systems
- Invisible from guest areas
- Enhanced security and climate control
- Deployment timeline: 10-14 days (utility coordination required)

### Option 3: Hybrid Approach
- Underground core systems (germination, cold chain, utility spine)
- Surface-adjacent harvesting access point
- Combines benefits of both approaches
