# Contributing to ABCFarmsIL

Thank you for your interest in contributing to ABCFarmsIL's regenerative agricultural infrastructure project. We welcome collaboration from engineers, researchers, agricultural specialists, and sustainability advocates.

---

## 🤝 Types of Contributions

We appreciate contributions in many forms:

### Technical Contributions
- System design improvements and optimizations
- Technical documentation and diagrams
- Code for control systems and monitoring
- Research on biological processes and aquaculture
- Supply chain research and vendor recommendations

### Documentation Contributions
- Clarifying existing documentation
- Translating documentation to other languages
- Creating visual aids (diagrams, flowcharts, schematics)
- Writing tutorials and guides

### Research Contributions
- Case studies and academic references
- Regulatory and compliance research
- Market analysis and business development
- Environmental impact assessments

### Testing & Validation
- Prototype testing and data collection
- Quality assurance procedures
- Performance benchmarking
- Failure mode analysis

## 🚀 Getting Started

### 1. Review the Project

Before contributing, please:
- Read the main [README.md](README.md)
- Understand the [buried infrastructure philosophy](docs/architecture/buried-infrastructure.md)
- Review the [three-zone water framework](docs/architecture/three-zone-water-framework.md)
- Explore system-specific documentation for relevant modules

### 2. Identify Your Area of Interest

Based on your expertise, focus on:
- **Engineers:** Technical specifications, BOM optimization, control systems
- **Researchers:** Biological processes, water quality, energy efficiency
- **Business Developers:** Market analysis, partnership proposals, ROI models
- **Regulatory Specialists:** Compliance documentation, permitting guidance

### 3. Contact the Team

For significant contributions, please contact the team first to:
- Discuss your proposed contribution
- Understand current priorities and roadmaps
- Avoid duplication of effort
- Ensure alignment with project goals

## 📝 Contribution Guidelines

### Technical Specifications

**When proposing system changes:**
1. Maintain the gravity-first design philosophy
2. Preserve the clean/dirty water separation
3. Consider replicability and scalability
4. Provide cost estimates where applicable
5. Include supplier information for Illinois market

**For BOM contributions:**
1. Update both module-specific and master BOM files
2. Include supplier class and priority information
3. Provide exact specifications and part numbers
4. Note any alternatives or substitutions
5. Include pricing when available

### Documentation

**Style guidelines:**
- Use clear, concise language
- Define technical acronyms on first use
- Include diagrams and visual aids where helpful
- Provide context for design decisions
- Link to related documentation

**Format:**
- Use Markdown for all documentation
- Include tables for specifications and data
- Use code blocks for lists and structured data
- Number steps in procedures
- Include diagrams as ASCII art or image references

### Research and Analysis

**When providing research:**
1. Cite sources with URLs
2. Include publication dates
3. Summarize key findings
4. Explain relevance to project
5. Note any limitations or caveats

**For market analysis:**
1. Define geographic scope (Illinois, Midwest, national)
2. Include data sources and methodology
3. Provide specific metrics and units
4. Discuss confidence intervals and margins of error
5. Reference industry standards and benchmarks

---

## 🧪 Testing and Validation

### Prototyping

**If proposing a prototype:**
1. Define success criteria clearly
2. Outline testing methodology
3. Identify required resources (time, materials, equipment)
4. Plan data collection and analysis
5. Consider failure modes and contingencies

### Data Collection

**When contributing data:**
1. Document data collection methods
2. Include calibration information for instruments
3. Note environmental conditions
4. Provide raw data and processed results
5. Explain any data normalization or transformation

---

## 🔍 Review Process

### Submission

1. Prepare your contribution following guidelines above
2. Include clear description of changes or additions
3. Highlight any dependencies or prerequisites
4. Note any potential conflicts or trade-offs
5. Submit to the ABCFarmsIL team for review

### Feedback Timeline

- **Initial acknowledgment:** Within 3 business days
- **Detailed review:** Within 10 business days
- **Decision and next steps:** Within 15 business days

### Types of Feedback

- **Approved:** Ready for integration
- **Approved with changes:** Minor revisions needed
- **Needs revision:** Significant changes required
- **Deferred:** Not currently a priority
- **Declined:** Does not align with project goals

## 📋 Code of Conduct

### Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors, regardless of:
- Experience level
- Background or discipline
- Geographic location
- Any other characteristic

### Expected Behavior

- **Respect:** Treat all contributors with respect
- **Collaboration:** Work constructively with others
- **Open-mindedness:** Consider diverse perspectives
- **Transparency:** Share intentions and reasoning
- **Accountability:** Take responsibility for contributions

### Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discrimination
- Dismissive or condescending language
- Taking credit for others' work
- Withholding information needed for collaboration
- Violating safety or ethical guidelines
