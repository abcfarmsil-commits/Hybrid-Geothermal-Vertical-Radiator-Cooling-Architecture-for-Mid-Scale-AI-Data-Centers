# ABCFARMSIL - Modular Infrastructure Systems

**Modular, repeatable infrastructure for regenerative systems, energy recovery, and controlled environments**

---

## Overview

ABCFARMSIL designs and documents modular infrastructure systems that can be deployed across multiple applications:

- **Food Production** - Indoor growing systems, aquaculture, controlled environments
- **Energy Recovery** - Gravity-based hydro battery modules, carbon offset infrastructure
- **Site Activation** - Temporary infrastructure for development-ready parcels
- **Data & Operations** - Predictive maintenance, telemetry, asset management

All systems share common principles: modularity, repeatability, infrastructure-grade reliability, and deterministic operation.

## Core Philosophy

### Modular Over Bespoke
- Standard containers and components
- Repeatable designs that scale through replication
- Avoids custom civil works where possible

### Gravity-First Operation
- Uses natural forces when possible
- Pumps as secondary, not primary
- Calm hydraulics, low velocity, low turbulence

### Infrastructure-Grade Behavior
- Deterministic, inspectable, predictable
- Long-life climate infrastructure
- Not experimental technology

### Carbon-First Framing
- Energy recovery, not generation
- Measurable carbon offsets
- Persistent, non-behavioral benefits

## Key Systems

### LUMENFIELD Hydro Battery Module
- **Purpose:** Gravity-based energy recovery from water movement
- **Performance:** 15-22 kWh/day recovered, 2-3 tonnes CO2e/year offset
- **Architecture:** Vertical containerized modules, 29m head, DN200 hydraulics
- **Positioning:** Long-life climate infrastructure, not energy generation

### Indoor Production Systems
- **Racks:** Modular 2-rack design, 4 lit shelves each, 24 trays total
- **Environment:** Controlled air movement, thermal control, dehumidification
- **Lighting:** LED fixtures with dimming/control
- **Water/Irrigation:** Reservoir, filtration, mist manifolds, leak detection

### Aquaculture Systems
- **Yellow Perch Breeding:** 53-foot reefer container design
- **Scope:** Breeding + hatchery + nursery core
- **Design:** Gravity-first, complete BOM, visual thermal management

### Site Activation Infrastructure
- **Purpose:** Activate underutilized parcels without permanent structures
- **Applications:** Brownfield sites, pre-certified industrial parcels, transitional zones
- **Benefits:** Real utility demand, grid-interactive load behavior, operational data
