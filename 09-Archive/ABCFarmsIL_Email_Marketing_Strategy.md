# ABCFarmsIL Email Marketing Strategy

### Primary Segments:
1. **High-End Restaurant Chefs**
   - Executive chefs at fine dining establishments
   - Sous chefs and kitchen managers

2. **Country Club & Hotel Culinary Teams**

   - Food & beverage directors at hotels

3. **Specialty Food Businesses**
   - Artisanal food producers
   - Specialty grocery stores
   - Meal kit companies

4. **Culinary Influencers**
   - Food writers and bloggers
   - Culinary instructors
   - Local food advocates

### Contact List Building:

1. **Research Methods:**
   - LinkedIn search for "Executive Chef" + "Chicago"
   - Restaurant association directories
   - Culinary school alumni networks
   - Food industry event attendee lists
   - Local restaurant review sites

2. **Information to Collect:**
   - Chef/contact name
   - Restaurant/business name

3. **Organization System:**
   - Create a spreadsheet with all contact information

## Email Campaign Sequence

### Email 1: Website Introduction
- **Purpose:** Drive traffic to new website
- **Timing:** Initial outreach
- **Call to Action:** Visit website
- **Follow-up:** After 3-4 days if no response

### Email 2: Value Proposition Deep Dive
- **Purpose:** Explain unique benefits in detail
- **Timing:** 5-7 days after Email 1
- **Call to Action:** Schedule a sample tasting
- **Follow-up:** After 5 days if no response

### Email 3: Chef Testimonial/Case Study
- **Purpose:** Build credibility with peer recommendations
- **Timing:** 7-10 days after Email 2
- **Call to Action:** Schedule a call or meeting
- **Follow-up:** After 5 days if no response

### Email 4: Special Offer
- **Purpose:** Create urgency with limited-time offer
- **Timing:** 7-10 days after Email 3
- **Call to Action:** Sign up for trial program
- **Follow-up:** Phone call within 3 days

## Email Best Practices

### Timing:
- Send emails Tuesday-Thursday for best open rates
- Optimal times: 10am-11am or 2pm-3pm
- Avoid Mondays and Fridays when possible
- Space emails appropriately (don't overwhelm)

### Technical Considerations:
- Use a professional email service (Mailchimp, Constant Contact, etc.)
- Ensure mobile responsiveness
- Keep images small and optimized
- Include plain-text versions
- Test all links before sending

### Personalization:
- Always use the chef's name
- Reference their restaurant or cuisine style when possible
- Mention specific menu items that could benefit from microgreens
- Customize subject lines for each segment

### Tracking & Analytics:
- Monitor open rates (industry average: 15-25%)
- Track click-through rates (industry average: 2-5%)
- Note which subject lines perform best
- Analyze which segments are most responsive
- Document all responses for follow-up

## Follow-Up Strategy

### For Email Opens (No Click):
- Send follow-up email with additional information
- Focus on different benefit in subject line
- Include more visual content

### For Email Clicks (No Response):
- Send follow-up email referencing their interest
- Offer specific next steps
- Provide phone number for direct contact

### For Partial Responses:
- Respond within 4 hours if possible
- Answer any questions thoroughly
- Always suggest next concrete step

### For Interested Prospects:
- Schedule sample delivery within 48 hours
- Send calendar invite for follow-up discussion
- Prepare customized variety recommendations

### For Non-Responsive Contacts:
- Try alternative contact method after 3 emails
- Consider direct mail with sample offer
- Leverage mutual connections when possible
