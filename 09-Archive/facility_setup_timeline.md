## Critical Path Analysis

### Critical Path Items
1. **Electrical modifications** - Required for most equipment installation
2. **Water system installation** - Required for irrigation and processing
3. **Growing rack assembly** - Foundation of production system
4. **Environmental control setup** - Essential for proper growing conditions
5. **Initial production test** - Final validation of all systems

### Potential Bottlenecks
1. **Permit approvals** - May delay structural modifications
2. **Electrical contractor availability** - May delay equipment installation
3. **Equipment delivery delays** - May impact installation timeline
4. **System integration issues** - May extend testing phase

## Contingency Plans

### Permit Delays
- **Plan A:** Begin non-permit-dependent work while waiting for approvals
- **Plan B:** Engage expediter to accelerate permit process
- **Timeline Impact:** Potential 1-2 week delay

### Equipment Delivery Delays
- **Plan A:** Identify alternative suppliers for critical items
- **Plan B:** Implement phased installation prioritizing available equipment
- **Timeline Impact:** Potential 1-3 week delay

### Contractor Availability Issues
- **Plan A:** Pre-book contractors with flexible scheduling
- **Plan B:** Identify backup contractors for critical work
- **Timeline Impact:** Potential 1-2 week delay

### System Integration Problems
- **Plan A:** Schedule additional testing time in timeline
- **Plan B:** Engage equipment vendor technical support
- **Timeline Impact:** Potential 1 week delay

## Budget Timeline

### Phase-Based Expenditure Plan
- **Pre-Setup Planning (Weeks 1-2):** 5% of total budget
- **Facility Preparation (Weeks 3-5):** 25% of total budget
- **Equipment Installation (Weeks 6-8):** 50% of total budget
- **System Testing (Weeks 9-10):** 10% of total budget
- **Initial Production (Weeks 11-12):** 10% of total budget

### Cash Flow Considerations
- Major equipment purchases should be timed to align with installation schedule
- Deposit payments (typically 50%) will be required for custom equipment
- Reserve 10% of budget for contingencies and unexpected expenses

## Appendix: Detailed Task Dependencies

This section contains detailed dependency maps for each major phase of the facility setup, showing the relationships between individual tasks and their impact on the overall timeline.

### Critical Dependencies Map
- Permit approvals → Structural modifications → Zone development → Equipment installation
- Electrical modifications → Lighting installation → Environmental control setup → System testing
- Water system installation → Irrigation setup → System testing → Initial production
- Growing rack assembly → Lighting installation → Initial production
- Processing area setup → Processing equipment installation → Workflow testing

### Parallel Work Opportunities
- Office setup can proceed in parallel with growing area development
- Security system installation can proceed in parallel with equipment setup
- SOP documentation can begin during system testing phase
- Staff training can begin during optimization phase
