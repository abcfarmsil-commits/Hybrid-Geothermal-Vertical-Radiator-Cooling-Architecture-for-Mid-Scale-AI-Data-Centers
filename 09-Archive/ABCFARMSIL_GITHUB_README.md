# ABCFarmsIL - Regenerative Agricultural Infrastructure

**Modular, gravity-first agricultural systems designed for climate resilience and scalable food production.**

---

## 🌱 Project Overview

ABCFarmsIL is developing containerized, modular agricultural infrastructure that combines traditional farming wisdom with modern engineering. Our systems are designed to:

- **Operate deterministically** like municipal utilities (water, power, storm infrastructure)
- **Scale through replication** rather than redesign
- **Prioritize gravity-first design** for reliability and reduced energy consumption
- **Integrate water, energy, and biological systems** in closed-loop architecture

### Core Philosophy

> **"Bury the system that cannot fail. Everything else is modular."**

We treat agricultural production as buried civic infrastructure, not farming. This approach:
- Aligns with municipal and subdivision infrastructure standards
- Enables statewide replication with local customization
- Reduces political, regulatory, and operational friction
- Future-proofs against biological and policy change

## 📦 System Portfolio

### 1. **Buried Infrastructure Core (Dome A)**
- Underground production core for reliable biomass and water resilience
- Non-pollinated plant production (microgreens, leafy greens)
- Integrated water capture, buffering, and treatment
- Infrastructure-level reliability without public dependency

### 2. **Fresh Algae PBR Platform (53-ft Reefer)**
- 5-lane vertical flat-panel photobioreactor system
- Fresh-product focus with 7-10 day shelf life
- Multi-flavor capability (Neutral Green, Umami, Bright, Deep Green, Seasonal)
- Cold-zone continuity from harvest through delivery

### 3. **Yellow Perch Hatchery Core (53-ft Reefer)**
- Breeding, spawning, egg incubation, larval rearing
- Gravity-first flow design (fish → solids → bio → sump → clean lift)
- 100% gravity drainage, pumps only for clean water lift
- Broodstock conditioning to early nursery stages

### 4. **Modular Surface Domes (Dome B - Optional)**
- Flexible, visible biology systems
- Protein systems, farmyard elements, education, research
- Independent ventilation and regulation
- Removable without affecting underground core

## 🏗️ Technical Architecture

### Three-Zone Water Framework

All systems follow a disciplined clean/dirty separation:

**Zone 1 - Clean Water:**
- Rain collection, filtration, UV sterilization
- Pressurized distribution for potable use
- Solids-free, nutrient-managed

**Zone 2 - Production Buffer:**
- Controlled exposure to biological systems
- Nutrient dosing for plant/fish growth
- Monitored quality parameters

**Zone 3 - Recovery & Biology:**
- Solids capture and removal
- Biological filtration
- Vermiculture and composting

### Design Principles

1. **Gravity-First:** Systems designed to flow downhill wherever possible
2. **Deterministic:** Mechanical reliability over biological variability
3. **Modular:** Standardized containers that replicate without redesign
4. **Inspector-Friendly:** Clear separation zones, visible flow paths
5. **Supply-Chain Resilient:** Regional suppliers, interchangeable components
