# IAM-Chi Financial Plan
## Indoor Agriculture Management - Chicago Program

## Executive Financial Summary

The IAM-Chi program represents a refined, focused approach to urban microgreens production that builds upon ABCFarmsIL's proven model. This comprehensive financial plan details the evolution from our original $69,000 seed funding request to a more sophisticated $9,000 IAM-Chi implementation, reflecting lessons learned and market validation.

### Key Financial Achievements
- **Reduced Initial Investment**: From $69,000 to $9,000 while maintaining production capacity
- **Accelerated ROI**: From 4.2 months to 3.0 months payback period
- **Enhanced Margins**: Improved gross margins through refined crop selection and operational efficiency
- **Scalable Framework**: Clear pathway from single module to multi-module network
