## **🎥 ABCFARMSIL — 90-Second VC Pitch Script**

**(0–10s | Identity)**  
Hi, I’m Jon James, founder of ABCFarmsIL.  
We build modular, high-margin food infrastructure designed to scale profitably in dense urban markets.

**(10–25s | Problem)**  
Urban food production is inefficient.  
It relies on long supply chains, high-capex facilities, and thin margins.  
That makes local food expensive, fragile, and hard to scale.

**(25–45s | Solution)**  
ABCFarmsIL solves this with standardized, modular grow units that deploy quickly, operate independently, and generate predictable cash flow.  
We start with microgreens because they turn inventory in days, not months — producing fast revenue and validating each site.

**(45–65s | Expansion \+ Moat)**  
Once a unit is cash-positive, the same infrastructure expands into mushrooms, soil systems, and additional modules.  
Our moat isn’t a single crop — it’s operational: SOPs, repeatable hardware, and a deployment model that scales location by location.

**(65–80s | Economics \+ Leverage)**  
We improve unit economics by reducing capex and labor risk through workforce and institutional partnerships — without giving up operational control.  
That accelerates profitability while keeping the business founder-led.

**(80–95s | Close)**  
ABCFarmsIL is scalable food infrastructure with real margins, real deployment, and real demand.  
We’re engaging partners and investors aligned with profitable, climate-resilient systems that work in the real world.
