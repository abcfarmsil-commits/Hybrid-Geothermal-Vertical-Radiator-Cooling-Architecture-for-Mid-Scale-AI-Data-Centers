# ABCFarmsIL — Modular Systems Packet (Phase 0.8–2)

## Purpose of This Packet
This packet is designed to explain ABCFarmsIL as a **set of independent, teachable production modules** that can operate alone or be tied together into a closed-loop system. It is written for **municipal partners, colleges, workforce programs, and technical reviewers**.

This is not a pitch deck. It is a **term‑paper‑level systems document** with clear inputs, outputs, roles, and baseline numbers.

---

## 1. System Overview (Big Picture)
**ABCFarmsIL is a modular micro‑farm infrastructure stack**, built from containers and controlled environments, designed for year‑round food production, workforce training, and local resilience.

Key principles:
- Modules must function independently
- Clean vs. dirty processes are physically separated
- Systems are measurable, teachable, and repeatable
- Energy, water, and nutrients are reused wherever possible

Phasing:
- **Phase 1:** Production + Harvest + Operator Cabin
- **Phase 2:** Fungi Science + Vermiculture
- **Phase 3:** Dome Production + Cuddles (on‑site installs)
- **Phase 4:** Aquatic / bait / specialty protein (future)

---

## 2. Module A — Operator Cabin & Irrigation Hub (Clean Side)
**Role:** Central control, power, water, data, and human operations

Functions:
- Water intake, filtration, and pressure management
- Irrigation distribution to all modules
- Power management (grid, solar, battery)
- Data logging, planning, and coordination

Key systems:
- Elevated water storage (thermal mass + gravity assist)
- Pumped and gravity-fed irrigation loops
- Monitoring laptop / dashboard (Sheets initially)

Outputs:
- Pressurized clean water
- Logged operational data
- Controlled distribution to grow modules

---

## 3. Module B — Microgreens Production ("Germination Engines")
**Role:** Fast-cycle, revenue-stabilizing crop production

Concept:
Microgreens trays are treated as **germination engines** — controlled biological reactors optimized for consistency and timing.

Key components:
- Vertical rack systems (rails or carts)
- LED light bars
- Top-down or mist irrigation
- Clean seeding and harvest workflow

Baseline metrics (illustrative):
- Cycle time: 7–14 days
- High turnover, predictable yield
- Low environmental variance

Why it matters:
- Immediate market fit
- Teachable systems
- Financial backbone of Phase 1

---

## 4. Module C — Harvest & Cooler Container
**Role:** Food safety, post-harvest handling, and logistics

Target environment:
- 42–44°F controlled zone
- Lower power demand than deep refrigeration

Layout concept:
- Central walkway
- Fold-down counters
- Hand-wash sink
- Utility / tool storage
- Air curtain + UV filtration between zones

Functions:
- Harvest tracking
- Cooling and short-term storage
- Packing and distribution staging

---

## 5. Module D — Fungi Science & Fruiting (Dirty Side)
**Role:** Protein-dense food production and soil inputs

Sub-modules:
### D1. Science Cubicle
- Substrate prep
- Augmentation storage
- Steam kettle (220°F)
- Sink and wet-work surfaces

Design intent:
- Minimize need for bulk autoclaving
- Emphasize hot soak + clean handling
- Compatible with liquid culture workflows

### D2. Fruiting Room
- Controlled temperature and humidity
- Rails or carts for bucket movement
- Misting and airflow management

Note:
Fruiting room is for **fruiting and harvesting**, not composting.

---

## 6. Module E — Vermiculture & Compost (Separate Space)
**Role:** Nutrient recovery and soil generation

Why separate:
- Biological heat
- Moisture
- Odor control
- Teaching clarity

Outputs:
- Worm castings
- Conditioned substrate inputs
- Soil amendments

Interfaces with:
- Fungi waste streams
- Dome production

---

## 7. Module F — Dome Production (End of Phase 2)
**Role:** Year‑round plant production + thermal engine

Key concept:
The dome is both a **growing environment** and a **thermal mass system**.

Features:
- Enclosed growing area
- Water tanks as heat sinks
- Circulating water loop
- Central irrigation head

System benefits:
- Temperature moderation
- Reduced energy inputs
- Enables power + water integration
