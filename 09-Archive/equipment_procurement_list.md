## Maintenance Considerations

### Regular Maintenance Schedule
- Daily: Clean harvesting tools, check environmental controls
- Weekly: Clean trays, check irrigation systems
- Monthly: Deep clean all equipment, check lighting systems
- Quarterly: Service refrigeration, replace filters

### Replacement Schedule
- Growing trays: Replace 10-15% annually
- Lighting: Replace bulbs/fixtures every 2-3 years
- Irrigation components: Inspect quarterly, replace as needed

## Notes on Equipment Selection
- All equipment selected meets commercial food safety standards
- Energy efficiency was prioritized for lighting and refrigeration
- Scalability considered for all major systems
- Automation incorporated where cost-effective
- Ergonomics considered for harvesting and processing equipment
