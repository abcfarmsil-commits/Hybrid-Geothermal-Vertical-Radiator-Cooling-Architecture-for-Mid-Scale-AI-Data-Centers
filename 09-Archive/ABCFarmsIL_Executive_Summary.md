# ABCFarmsIL Executive Summary

## Business Overview
ABCFarmsIL is an innovative microgreens operation focused on serving professional chefs with ultra-fresh, premium quality microgreens through a unique 6-square system. Our mission, "Always Be Cultivating," drives us to provide chefs with the freshest, most flavorful microgreens harvested hours before delivery to elevate their culinary creations.

## Unique Value Proposition
1. **Ultra-Fresh Delivery**: From growing room to kitchen in hours, not days
2. **Proprietary 6-Square System**: Multiple varieties in one convenient tray with attached scissors
3. **Chef-Driven Production**: Custom growing based on chef needs and preferences
4. **Intensified Flavor**: Up to 40x more concentrated than mature plants

## Market Opportunity
The specialty food market in the US reached $175 billion in 2023, with the microgreens segment projected to grow at 13.5% annually through 2030. Our immediate serviceable available market includes approximately 250 potential chef clients within our local delivery radius, representing a potential annual revenue of $2.7 million for microgreens alone.

## Business Model
- **Subscription-Based**: Weekly delivery of 4-8 trays per chef
- **Premium Pricing**: $35-50 per tray depending on varieties and volume
- **Strong Unit Economics**: 55% gross margin with $22 profit per tray
- **Recurring Revenue**: $210-$300 weekly revenue per chef client

## Financial Highlights
- **Break-even Point**: Month 2 (60 trays/month)
- **Rapid Profitability**: Cash-positive in Month 3
- **Self-Funded Expansion**: Phase 1 expansion in Month 4
- **5-Year Revenue Projection**: $1.08 million by Year 5
- **Profit Margin**: Scaling from 9.3% in Year 1 to 24% by Year 5
