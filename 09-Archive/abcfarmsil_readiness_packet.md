## 🟩 Redevelopment Framework & Build Phasing

### Vision
Create a closed-loop, solar-powered urban micro-farm demonstrating energy, water, and nutrient circularity through container architecture and CEJA-aligned monitoring.

### Program Elements
* **Phase 1B – Microgreens Module (IAM funded)**   Rack expansion · gravity mist loop · sensor suite · portable infrastructure assembly.
* **Phase 2 – Fungi + Science Module**   53 ft reefer conversion · clean workflow · cold chain stabilization.
* **Dome Integration – Regen Loop Activation**   Solar canopy · rainwater capture · soil and compost tower tie-in.

### CEJA / Utility Alignment
* Demand-response ready LED and HVAC zones.  
* IAQ sensors log CO₂/RH/temp for MRV reporting.  
* Electrified food node compatible with ComEd pilot metrics.
