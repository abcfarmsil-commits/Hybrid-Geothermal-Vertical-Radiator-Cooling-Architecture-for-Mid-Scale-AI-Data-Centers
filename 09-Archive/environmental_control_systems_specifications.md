# Environmental Control Systems Specifications
## ABCFarmsIL Microgreens Production Facility

## 1. Executive Summary

This document outlines the comprehensive environmental control systems specifications for ABCFarmsIL's 2,500 sq ft microgreens production facility. The environmental control systems are critical to maintaining optimal growing conditions for consistent, high-quality microgreens production year-round. The specifications cover HVAC requirements, lighting systems, irrigation/watering systems, and monitoring/automation requirements for each zone of the facility.

## 2. Facility Zoning & Environmental Requirements

The facility is divided into five primary zones, each with specific environmental control requirements:

### 2.1 Receiving & Pre-Production Zone (400 sq ft)
| Parameter | Specification | Notes |
|-----------|--------------|-------|
| Temperature | 65-70°F (18-21°C) | Standard room temperature |
| Humidity | 40-60% RH | Prevent seed/media deterioration |
| Air Exchange | 4-6 ACH | Maintain air quality |
| Lighting | 300-500 lux | Standard workspace lighting |

### 2.2 Germination Zone (400 sq ft)
| Parameter | Specification | Notes |
|-----------|--------------|-------|
| Temperature | 70-75°F (21-24°C) | Higher temp promotes germination |
| Humidity | 80-90% RH | High humidity for seed sprouting |
| Air Exchange | 3-4 ACH | Limited air movement |
| Lighting | Minimal/None | Darkness promotes germination |

### 2.3 Growing Zone (1,000 sq ft)
| Parameter | Specification | Notes |
|-----------|--------------|-------|
| Temperature | 65-70°F (18-21°C) | Optimal for most microgreens |
| Humidity | 40-60% RH | Prevent disease, promote growth |
| Air Exchange | 6-8 ACH | Prevent disease, provide CO₂ |
| Lighting | See Section 3 | Specific lighting requirements |

### 2.4 Harvesting & Processing Zone (400 sq ft)
| Parameter | Specification | Notes |
|-----------|--------------|-------|
| Temperature | 60-65°F (15-18°C) | Cooler for harvested product |
| Humidity | 50-60% RH | Maintain product freshness |
| Air Exchange | 8-10 ACH | Food safety requirement |
| Lighting | 750-1000 lux | Task lighting for processing |

### 2.5 Shipping & Storage Zone (300 sq ft)
| Parameter | Specification | Notes |
|-----------|--------------|-------|
| Temperature | 33-38°F (1-3°C) | Cold storage for finished product |
| Humidity | 90-95% RH | Maintain product freshness |
| Air Exchange | 2-3 ACH | Minimal for energy efficiency |
| Lighting | 300-500 lux | Standard workspace lighting |
