Bamboo stakes will be valuable for tall microgreens varieties:

**Recommended Applications:**

- **Sunflower greens:** May need support in later growth stages
- **Wheatgrass:** Benefits from support to prevent lodging

**Support Strategy:**
- Install stakes at planting time for crops known to need support
- Use twist ties to gently secure plants without damage
- Adjust stake height as plants grow
- Remove at harvest to avoid contamination
