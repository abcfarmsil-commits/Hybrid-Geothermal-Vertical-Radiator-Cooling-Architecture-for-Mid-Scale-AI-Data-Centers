## 🎯 YOUR QUESTION: "DO YOU MEAN MATS?"

### YES - Multiple Growing Media Options!

** tray = container, NOT just one planting material**

You have **FIVE media types** to test:

#### **1. Hemp Grow Mats** (100 initial purchase)
- **Pros:** Clean, professional, easy to handle, consistent results
- **Cons:** Higher cost, single-use typically
- **Best for:** Premium markets, restaurants, high-margin crops
- **Cost per tray:** ~$2-3 per mat
- **Usage:** 40-60 trays per week

#### **2. Coconut Coir** (20 bricks = ~100-150 tray equivalents)
- **Pros:** Versatile, good drainage, reusable, cost-effective
- **Cons:** Requires preparation (hydration), can vary by batch
- **Best for:** Testing, cost optimization, variety crops
- **Cost per tray:** ~$0.50-1.00 per tray
- **Usage:** 20-40 trays per week

#### **3. Burlap Sheets** (50 initial purchase)
- **Pros:** Natural fiber, good for certain crops, rustic appeal
- **Cons:** Can retain too much water, variable quality
- **Best for:** Specialty crops, testing, aesthetic markets
- **Cost per tray:** ~$1.50 per sheet
- **Usage:** 10-20 trays per week

#### **4. Vermiculite + Perlite Mix** (soilless mix)
- **Pros:** Traditional, familiar to growers, good water retention
- **Cons:** Messy, heavier, harder to clean trays
- **Best for:** Root crops, experimental varieties
- **Cost per tray:** ~$1.00-1.50 per tray
- **Usage:** 5-10 trays per week

#### **5. No Media (Hydroponic/NFT)** 
- **Pros:** Cleanest, reusable, professional
- **Cons:** Requires specialized setup, higher initial cost
- **Best for:** Large-scale production, standardized crops
- **Cost per tray:** ~$0 (after initial setup)
- **Usage:** Future expansion, not Phase 0.8

## 🎯 KEY INSIGHTS:

### 1. **Capacity ≠ Production**
- 512 trays = **maximum capacity**
- Realistic production = **150-250 active trays**
- Weekly planting/harvest = **30-50 trays**

### 2. **Media Testing is Strategic**
- Start with **3 media types** for 5 core varieties
- Document results meticulously
- Scale up with proven combinations
- Eliminate underperformers quickly

### 3. **Workflow Efficiency Matters**
- Don't fill all 512 trays
- Maintain **buffer zones** for workflow
- Allow time for cleaning, maintenance, problem-solving
- Scale production gradually as you optimize

### 4. **The "500 Item" Inventory Strategy**
- **Doesn't mean 500 different seeds**
- **Means 500+ inventory items total:**
  - 50-100 seed varieties
  - 5-10 media types
  - 20-30 seed quantities (bulk vs. trial sizes)
  - 100-200 trays
  - 100+ growing supplies (labels, tools, etc.)
  - 50-100 packaging materials
