### ✅ System Overview
Phase 0.8 system will validate pressurized hand-spray irrigation loops for one to two mobile racks. Each rack operates with low-pressure misting using 1/4″ drip components, food-safe tubing, and quick-connect plumbing. Gravity assist is minimal; pressure maintained by hand-pump sprayer (~30 PSI).  

**Target outcomes:**
- Closed, food-safe, sanitizable loop
- Modular components (scalable to container and fixed installations)
- Compatibility with MRV data capture and IoT sensors

### 🧪 Validation & Testing
- **Pressure test:** Hold 30 PSI for 90 sec (no visible leaks)
- **Flush test:** Activate drain valve; observe flow rate and backwash
- **Sanitation:** Circulate H₂O₂ solution (1:10) weekly through flush valve
- **MRV data tag:** Attach QR code (BETCKEY DK-2210 label + Aegis film overlay) to each shelf
