# ABCFarmsIL Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

## Overview
This document outlines the key performance indicators (KPIs) that ABCFarmsIL should track to measure business success, operational efficiency, and progress toward strategic goals. These metrics are organized by business function and include target values, measurement methods, and reporting frequencies.

## 1. Financial Performance KPIs

### Revenue Metrics
| KPI | Description | Target | Measurement Method | Reporting Frequency |
|-----|-------------|--------|-------------------|---------------------|
| **Monthly Revenue** | Total revenue generated each month | Year 1: $8K-10K/month<br>Year 3: $23K/month | Financial reporting system | Monthly |
| **Revenue Growth Rate** | Month-over-month percentage increase in revenue | 5-10% monthly (early stage) | (Current month - Previous month) / Previous month | Monthly |
| **Revenue per Tray** | Average revenue generated per tray sold | $28-37 per tray | Total revenue / Number of trays sold | Weekly |
| **Revenue by Client Segment** | Revenue breakdown by client type (fine dining, hotels, etc.) | No specific target; for analysis | Segment revenue / Total revenue | Monthly |

### Profitability Metrics
| KPI | Description | Target | Measurement Method | Reporting Frequency |
|-----|-------------|--------|-------------------|---------------------|
| **Gross Margin** | Percentage of revenue remaining after COGS | 55-60% | (Revenue - COGS) / Revenue | Monthly |
| **Net Profit Margin** | Percentage of revenue remaining after all expenses | Year 1: 5-10%<br>Year 3: 15-20% | Net profit / Total revenue | Monthly |
| **Contribution Margin per Tray** | Profit contribution of each tray after variable costs | $19-28 per tray | Tray revenue - Variable costs per tray | Weekly |
| **Break-even Analysis** | Number of trays needed to cover fixed costs | 60-70 trays/month | Fixed costs / Contribution margin per tray | Monthly |

### Cash Flow Metrics
| KPI | Description | Target | Measurement Method | Reporting Frequency |
|-----|-------------|--------|-------------------|---------------------|
| **Cash Runway** | Months of operation possible with current cash | Minimum 3 months | Cash on hand / Monthly burn rate | Weekly |
| **Days Sales Outstanding** | Average time to collect payment from clients | <15 days | (Accounts receivable / Revenue) × 365 | Monthly |
| **Burn Rate** | Rate at which company is spending cash reserves | Decreasing month-over-month | Cash spent per month | Monthly |
| **Working Capital Ratio** | Ability to pay short-term obligations | >1.5 | Current assets / Current liabilities | Monthly |

## 2. Operational Performance KPIs

### Production Metrics
| KPI | Description | Target | Measurement Method | Reporting Frequency |
|-----|-------------|--------|-------------------|---------------------|
| **Production Volume** | Number of trays produced monthly | Phase 0.8: 100 trays/month<br>Phase 1: 250 trays/month | Production tracking system | Weekly |
| **Production Capacity Utilization** | Percentage of maximum capacity being used | >85% | Actual production / Maximum capacity | Weekly |
| **Germination Success Rate** | Percentage of seeds successfully germinating | >95% | Successful trays / Total trays seeded | Daily |
| **Growing Cycle Time** | Days from seeding to harvest | 7-14 days (variety dependent) | Date tracking per tray | Per batch |
| **Yield per Tray** | Average weight/volume harvested per tray | Variety-specific targets | Weight measurement at harvest | Per harvest |

### Quality Metrics
| KPI | Description | Target | Measurement Method | Reporting Frequency |
|-----|-------------|--------|-------------------|---------------------|
| **Product Quality Score** | Rating of harvested microgreens (1-5 scale) | >4.5 average | Quality assessment at harvest | Per harvest |
| **Shelf Life Performance** | Days product remains fresh after harvest | >7 days | Shelf life testing | Weekly |
| **Quality Rejection Rate** | Percentage of product rejected for quality issues | <2% | Rejected product / Total production | Weekly |
| **Chef Satisfaction Score** | Rating from chefs on product quality (1-5) | >4.8 average | Chef feedback surveys | Monthly |
| **Consistency Rating** | Evaluation of product consistency between batches | >4.5 out of 5 | Quality control assessment | Weekly |

### Efficiency Metrics
| KPI | Description | Target | Measurement Method | Reporting Frequency |
|-----|-------------|--------|-------------------|---------------------|
| **Labor Hours per Tray** | Time spent on production per tray | <15 minutes per tray | Time tracking system | Weekly |
| **Energy Usage per Tray** | kWh consumed per tray produced | <0.5 kWh per tray | Energy monitoring system | Monthly |
| **Water Usage per Tray** | Gallons of water used per tray | <0.5 gallons per tray | Water metering | Monthly |
| **Seed Usage Efficiency** | Yield relative to seed input | >90% of theoretical yield | Yield / Expected yield from seed | Weekly |
| **Waste Percentage** | Percentage of inputs that become waste | <5% | Waste weight / Total input weight | Weekly |

### Logistics Metrics
| KPI | Description | Target | Measurement Method | Reporting Frequency |
|-----|-------------|--------|-------------------|---------------------|
| **On-Time Delivery Rate** | Percentage of deliveries made within promised window | >98% | On-time deliveries / Total deliveries | Weekly |
| **Delivery Cost per Tray** | Average cost to deliver each tray | <$2 per tray | Total delivery costs / Number of trays | Weekly |
| **Tray Return Rate** | Percentage of trays returned for reuse | >95% | Returned trays / Delivered trays | Weekly |
| **Route Efficiency** | Deliveries completed per hour | >4 deliveries per hour | Deliveries / Delivery hours | Weekly |
| **Average Delivery Distance** | Average miles traveled per delivery | <5 miles | Total miles / Number of deliveries | Weekly |

## KPI Dashboard Implementation

### Dashboard Structure
1. **Executive Summary Dashboard**
   - Top-level KPIs across all categories
   - Month-over-month and year-over-year comparisons
   - Progress toward strategic goals

2. **Financial Dashboard**
   - Detailed revenue and profitability metrics
   - Cash flow indicators
   - Comparison to budget and forecasts

3. **Operations Dashboard**
   - Production volumes and efficiency
   - Quality metrics
   - Logistics performance

4. **Sales & Marketing Dashboard**
   - Client acquisition and retention
   - Marketing campaign performance
   - Sales pipeline health

5. **Sustainability & Impact Dashboard**
   - Environmental metrics
   - Social impact indicators
   - Innovation progress

### Data Collection Methods
1. **Automated Data Collection**
   - Production tracking system integration
   - Financial software integration
   - CRM data synchronization
   - IoT sensors for environmental monitoring

2. **Manual Data Entry**
   - Quality assessments
   - Client feedback
   - Team performance evaluations
   - Innovation tracking

3. **Scheduled Reporting**
   - Daily production reports
   - Weekly sales updates
   - Monthly financial statements
   - Quarterly strategic reviews

### Dashboard Technology Options
1. **Initial Implementation (Year 1)**
   - Google Sheets with connected tabs
   - Simple visualization using built-in charts
   - Manual updates on set schedule

2. **Intermediate Solution (Year 2)**
   - Microsoft Power BI or Google Data Studio
   - Semi-automated data connections
   - Scheduled refreshes and alerts

3. **Advanced Solution (Year 3+)**
   - Custom dashboard with API integrations
   - Real-time data visualization
   - Predictive analytics capabilities

## KPI Review Process

### Weekly Review
- **Participants:** Core team
- **Focus:** Operational KPIs, sales pipeline, production metrics
- **Outcome:** Tactical adjustments to weekly activities

### Monthly Review
- **Participants:** Management team
- **Focus:** Financial performance, client acquisition, quality metrics
- **Outcome:** Resource allocation decisions, monthly priorities

### Quarterly Strategic Review
- **Participants:** Management team and advisors
- **Focus:** Progress toward strategic goals, sustainability metrics, innovation
- **Outcome:** Strategic adjustments, quarterly objectives

### Annual KPI Evaluation
- **Participants:** All stakeholders
- **Focus:** Comprehensive review of all KPIs
- **Outcome:** KPI refinement, target adjustments, new metrics as needed

## Conclusion
This KPI framework provides ABCFarmsIL with a comprehensive system for measuring business performance across all critical areas. By consistently tracking these metrics, the company can make data-driven decisions, identify areas for improvement, and demonstrate progress to stakeholders. The framework should be reviewed and refined annually to ensure it continues to align with strategic objectives and business evolution.
