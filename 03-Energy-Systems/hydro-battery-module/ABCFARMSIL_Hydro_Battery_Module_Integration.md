## Why Hydro Battery Module Belongs Underground

### Design Characteristics Match Underground Rules:

1. **Sealed Systems**
   - Containerized vertical modules
   - Fully buried installation
   - No outdoor access required

2. **Deterministic Operation**
   - Gravity-first, pump-second operation
   - Predictable energy recovery (0.05-0.06 kWh/m³)
   - Inspectable, infrastructure-grade behavior

3. **Non-Pollinated / Non-Biological**
   - Purely hydraulic and electrical systems
   - No biological contamination risk
   - No competition with biological systems

4. **Invisible / NIMBY-Proof**
   - Underground burial eliminates visual impact
   - Quiet operation (calm hydraulics)
   - Infrastructure easement deployment

5. **Utility-Grade**
   - Carbon offset infrastructure (2-3 tonnes CO₂e/year)
   - Grid demand reduction
   - Site resilience and emergency backup

### Technical Integration Specifications

**Hydraulic Integration:**
- **Connection Point:** Utility Spine water management system
- **Flow Rate:** 300-360 m³/day cycled volume
- **Pipe Size:** DN200 primary
- **Velocity:** ≤1.5 m/s (calm hydraulics)
- **Head:** ~29 m total (4 below-grade loops @ 6m + 1 above-grade @ 5m)

**Energy Integration:**
- **Recovery:** 15-22 kWh/day per module
- **Power:** 0.6-0.9 kW continuous equivalent
- **Peak:** 2-4 kW during active flow
- **Application:** Lighting, controls, slow EV charging

**MRV Integration:**
- **Carbon Offset:** 2-3 tonnes CO₂e/year (conservative grid intensity 0.4 kg CO₂e/kWh)
- **Persistence:** Additional, non-behavioral, infrastructure-linked
- **Tracking:** Integrated into ABCFARMSIL MRV metrics dictionary
