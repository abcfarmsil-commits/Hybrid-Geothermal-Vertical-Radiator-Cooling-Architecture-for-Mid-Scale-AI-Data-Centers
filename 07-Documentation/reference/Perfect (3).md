# **1️⃣ SHISO (Perilla frutescens)**

### **Market Position**

* Japanese, Korean, fusion cuisine staple  
* Extremely hard to source fresh in Midwest  
* Often imported or low-quality when available  
* Strong sushi \+ upscale restaurant demand

### **Pricing (regional typical)**

* $10–20 per 4 oz clamshell (retail equivalent)  
* $20–30 per lb wholesale possible  
* High turnover potential

### **Production Profile**

* 6–8 week maturity  
* Tolerates rack-level grow room  
* Moderate water needs  
* No high humidity requirement  
* Compatible with microgreen-style workflow

### **Why It Fits ABCFARMSIL**

* Small footprint  
* Repeated harvest possible  
* Strong chef leverage tool  
* High margin relative to sq ft  
* No system redesign required

# **2️⃣ KAFFIR LIME (Makrut) LEAVES**

### **Market Position**

* Essential in Thai and SE Asian cuisine  
* Almost never available truly fresh locally  
* Imported leaves lose aroma quickly

### **Pricing**

* $5–10 per small retail bundle  
* High per-ounce value  
* Low weight, high aroma density

### **Production Profile**

* Perennial shrub  
* Container-grown  
* Moderate light  
* Low harvest frequency  
* Low labor

### **Why It Fits**

* Long-term rack corner crop  
* Doesn’t disrupt production rhythm  
* High differentiation  
* Extremely strong chef acquisition angle

* $4–8 per small bunch

### **Production Profile**

* Perennial shrub  
* Container-compatible  
* Prefers warm controlled environment  
* Low irrigation sensitivity

### **Strategic Advantage**

* Ethnic restaurant leverage  
* CSA differentiation  
* Low space demand
