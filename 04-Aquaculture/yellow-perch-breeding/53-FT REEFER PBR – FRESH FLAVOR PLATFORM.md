## **Zone A – 5-lane PBR layout (core of the system)**

### **PBR type**

**Vertical flat-panel PBRs**, double-sided LED, food-grade polymer or glass.

### **Lane structure (repeatable module)**

* **5 lanes**  
* **Each lane \= 2 panels**  
* **\~120 L per panel**

**Total panels:** 10  
**Total working volume:** \~1,200 L

Panels are mounted:

* Parallel to container length  
* With a central service aisle  
* Overhead gas \+ nutrient manifolds

---

### **Lane isolation (critical, and solved)**

Each lane has:

* Dedicated media feed  
* Dedicated CO₂ injection  
* Dedicated harvest outlet  
* Shared but valved CIP return

No flavor bleed. No operational gymnastics.
