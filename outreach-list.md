## Outreach Strategy

### Week 1: Technical Validation
- Contact 3-5 manufacturers (Tier 1)
- Goal: Get feedback on manufacturability, identify fatal flaws

### Week 2: Pilot Host Search
- Contact 5-10 data center operators (Tier 2)
- Contact 3-5 industrial/campus facilities (Tier 4)
- Goal: Find one host willing to evaluate pilot

### Week 3: Engineering Partners
- Contact 3-5 MEP/engineering firms (Tier 3)
- Goal: Understand specification path, get professional feedback

### Success Metrics
- 15-20 emails sent
- 3-5 conversations started
- 1 manufacturer willing to evaluate
- 1 potential pilot host identified
